What we handle

Operational support built around Costco receiving requirements.

Grey Wolf can sit between your production flow and your Costco delivery window so shipments leave cleaner, better labeled, and easier to receive.

EDI and shipment data

We work alongside your EDI provider or internal team so packing details, counts, and outbound shipment information line up before the load ships.

ASN timing and accuracy

Grey Wolf helps keep ASN-related shipment details clean and aligned to reduce receiving friction once freight reaches the retailer network.

Labels and pallet prep

We support pallet label placement, freight presentation, count verification, and relabeling or repalletizing when needed.

Process

A simpler flow for Costco-bound freight.

1

Review the retailer instructions

We confirm the routing guide, pallet setup, ASN timing expectations, and label requirements before the shipment is staged.

2

Prep the freight

Grey Wolf verifies counts, relabels if required, and cleans up pallet presentation so outbound freight is retailer-ready.

3

Coordinate the outbound move

We align with the shipping plan, appointment timing, and carrier handoff so the outbound window is easier to manage.

4

Support exceptions fast

If counts change, labels need rework, or freight needs cleanup before delivery, Grey Wolf can respond quickly from the GTA.

FAQ

Common questions about Costco support.

Do you provide EDI software?

No. Grey Wolf supports the warehouse and shipment-prep side of retailer compliance and can coordinate with your EDI provider or internal team.

Can Grey Wolf fix label or pallet issues before delivery?

Yes. We can support relabeling, repalletizing, count checks, and freight cleanup when shipments need work before they move to the retailer.

Can you support ongoing Costco replenishment as well as one-off projects?

Yes. Grey Wolf can support repeat retailer flows, seasonal surges, and special projects that need warehouse staging and compliance prep.
