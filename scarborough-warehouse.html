Warehousing in Scarborough

Our Scarborough warehouse supports businesses across Toronto's east end with reliable storage, fulfillment and cross-docking.

Why Choose a Scarborough 3PL?

Scarborough's proximity to Highway 401 and the growing communities of East Toronto makes it a strategic location for warehousing. Grey Wolf's facility offers secure racking, bulk storage and advanced inventory management to keep your products safe and organised. Our team processes inbound shipments quickly and ensures orders are fulfilled accurately and on time.

We provide same-day shipping to local customers and efficient distribution across the GTA through our network of carriers. Our cross-docking and transloading capabilities reduce handling time for freight destined for other Canadian provinces or the U.S. In addition, we offer value-added services such as kitting, labelling and custom packaging to meet your branding needs.

As part of our 3PL solution, we manage returns, provide refurbishment and handle customs documentation for cross-border shipments. When you partner with Grey Wolf in Scarborough, you gain a reliable logistics team dedicated to helping your business grow.

  • Pick, pack & ship services with same-day options
  • Cross-docking and transloading to minimise delays

  • Returns management and refurbishment
