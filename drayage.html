Ontario drayage FAQ

Questions customers usually ask before booking.

These quick answers help buyers and dispatch teams understand where Grey Wolf fits before sending a drayage request.

What drayage moves does Grey Wolf handle in Ontario?

Grey Wolf supports container, terminal, port, rail, and local drayage moves across Mississauga, the Greater Toronto Area, and Ontario.

Can drayage be combined with warehousing or cross-docking?

Yes. Grey Wolf can connect drayage to warehousing, cross-docking, transload, unload, and short-term storage when the load needs more than pickup and delivery.

The fastest quote path is the contact name, phone number, load type, reference, requested service date, and the complete ship-from and ship-to addresses.
