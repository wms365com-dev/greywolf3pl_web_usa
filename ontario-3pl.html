Ontario 3PL & Logistics Services

From warehousing to distribution and value-added services, Grey Wolf supports your supply chain across Ontario.

Why Choose a Ontario 3PL?

Ontario's vast geography and diverse economy require a flexible logistics partner. Grey Wolf operates a network of warehouses throughout the GTA and Southern Ontario, providing storage, fulfillment and cross-docking to businesses of all sizes. Our facilities are strategically located near major highways, ports and border crossings, enabling efficient distribution across Canada and to the U.S.

Whether you need short-term overflow space or long-term contract warehousing, our scalable solutions grow with your business. We offer pick and pack fulfillment, kitting, labelling and custom packaging, as well as cross-docking and transloading to keep freight moving. Our warehouse management technology provides real-time visibility and integration with your eCommerce and ERP platforms.

For cross-border shipments, Grey Wolf handles customs documentation, duty payments and carrier co-ordination. Our experienced team can help you navigate regulations, tax implications and brokerage processes to ensure your goods arrive on schedule. Trust us as your Ontario 3PL partner to optimise your supply chain and deliver outstanding customer service.

Our Services in Ontario

  • Scalable warehousing across multiple Ontario locations
  • Pick, pack & ship for eCommerce, retail and industrial clients
  • Cross-docking and transloading to reduce dwell time
  • Kitting, labelling and custom packaging services
  • Customs compliance and cross-border logistics support

Cross-Border Logistics

As part of our Ontario 3PL services, we specialise in cross-border shipping. From duty payments to carrier selection, we manage every step to ensure seamless exports to the U.S.
