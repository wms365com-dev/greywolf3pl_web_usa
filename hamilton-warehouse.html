Warehousing in Hamilton

Our Hamilton facility delivers secure storage, fast fulfillment and cross-docking with direct access to the port and highways.

Why Choose a Hamilton 3PL?

Hamilton's thriving manufacturing sector and port facilities make it a strategic logistics hub. Our warehouse offers secure racking, bulk storage and temperature-controlled options to support a range of industries. Located near the QEW and Highway 403, we enable efficient distribution across Southern Ontario and to cross-border destinations.

Our Hamilton team excels at pick and pack fulfillment, cross-docking and transloading. We manage inbound and outbound shipments through the Port of Hamilton, CN and CP rail terminals and major highways. By integrating our warehouse management systems with your order platforms, we ensure real-time visibility and accuracy.

Grey Wolf also provides value-added services such as kitting, labelling and custom packaging. When exporting to the U.S., we handle customs documentation and co-ordinate carriers. Choose our Hamilton facility to optimise your supply chain and reach markets across Canada and the U.S.

Our Services in Hamilton

  • Secure racking, bulk and temperature-controlled storage
  • Pick, pack & ship for all order volumes
  • Cross-docking and transloading at the port and rail terminals
  • Kitting, labelling and packaging customisation
  • Returns processing and refurbishment

Cross-Border Access

With convenient access to the Buffalo and Detroit border crossings, our Hamilton facility simplifies your exports to the U.S. through comprehensive customs and logistics support.
