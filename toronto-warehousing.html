Warehousing in Toronto

Our Toronto facility offers scalable warehousing, order fulfillment and cross-docking just minutes from the city's downtown core.

Why Choose a Toronto 3PL?

Toronto is a dynamic city with a thriving retail and eCommerce sector. Operating from a local warehouse reduces last-mile delivery times and provides easier access to the port of Toronto, Pearson Airport and major rail terminals. At Grey Wolf, we combine prime urban warehousing with robust inventory systems to support both small businesses and large enterprises.

When you choose our Toronto warehouse, you gain a team dedicated to accuracy and speed. We receive your goods, store them in secured racking, and process orders with meticulous care. Same-day and next-day deliveries within the city keep your customers satisfied, while our cross-docking services minimise handling and shorten transit for time-sensitive shipments.

Our value-added services enable you to customise packaging, bundle products and manage returns, all from one location. For companies importing goods through the Port of Toronto or shipping to the U.S., we handle customs documentation and leverage our carrier relationships for competitive rates. Grow your Toronto presence with a logistics partner that understands the city's unique challenges and opportunities.

Our Services in Toronto

  • Urban warehousing with secure rack and bulk storage
  • eCommerce pick, pack & ship services with same-day options
  • Cross-docking to reduce handling and speed up distribution
  • Custom packaging, kitting and labelling for retail readiness
  • Returns processing and refurbishment to delight customers

Cross-Border Services

Located within an hour of the U.S. border, our Toronto facility provides customs-cleared warehousing and integrated shipping solutions for exports to the U.S. We coordinate brokerage, duty payment and final mile delivery.
