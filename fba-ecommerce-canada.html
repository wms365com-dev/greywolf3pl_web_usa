FBA & eCommerce Fulfillment in Canada

Sell confidently in Canada with our FBA prep and eCommerce fulfillment services. We ensure your products meet Amazon's requirements and arrive quickly to Canadian customers.

Why Choose a Canada 3PL?

Selling online in Canada means you need a partner who understands Amazon's strict FBA requirements as well as the expectations of Canadian consumers. Grey Wolf offers comprehensive FBA prep services: inspection, labeling, bundling and packaging to ensure your products meet Amazon's guidelines and avoid compliance penalties. Once prepared, we ship your goods directly to Amazon fulfillment centres across Canada or to your customers through our eCommerce fulfillment services.

Our facilities provide secure storage, real-time inventory management and integration with major eCommerce platforms like Shopify, WooCommerce and Amazon Seller Central. We pick, pack and ship orders quickly, using optimized packaging to reduce costs and protect your products. Our returns processing services include inspection, refurbishment and restocking to minimize losses and keep your inventory accurate.

For cross-border sellers, we manage customs documentation and duty payments for shipments entering Canada from the U.S. Our multi-carrier network ensures competitive shipping rates and reliable delivery across provinces. With Grey Wolf handling your FBA prep and eCommerce fulfillment, you can focus on product development, marketing and customer engagement while we manage the logistics.

  • Secure warehousing and real-time inventory management
