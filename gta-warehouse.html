Warehousing Across the GTA

Our 3PL facilities across the Greater Toronto Area (GTA) provide secure storage, fast fulfillment and cross-docking services to keep your supply chain moving.

Why Choose a GTA 3PL?

The Greater Toronto Area is Canada's largest economic hub, making it critical for businesses to have local warehousing and distribution capabilities. Partnering with a GTA 3PL like Grey Wolf means you benefit from proximity to millions of customers, major highways (401, 400, 404), and key cross-border routes. Our strategic network reduces transit times and shipping costs while giving you flexibility in handling peak seasons and returns management.

With facilities near Toronto Pearson International Airport and CN/CP rail terminals, we can seamlessly integrate air, rail and ground transport for import and export shipments. Our experienced team manages inventory from receipt to final mile delivery, providing real-time visibility through our warehouse management system. Whether you're an eCommerce brand seeking same-day delivery in Toronto or a manufacturer requiring bulk pallet storage, we tailor our services to your volumes and products.

Beyond warehousing, we offer value-added services such as kitting, labelling, custom packaging and returns processing. For companies shipping to the U.S., our cross-border expertise ensures your GTA shipments clear customs quickly and arrive on time. By entrusting your logistics to Grey Wolf, you can focus on growing your business while we handle the complexity of distribution across the GTA and beyond.

Our Services in GTA

  • Secure pallet and bulk storage with flexible terms
  • Pick, pack & ship for eCommerce and retail orders
  • Cross-docking & transloading to speed up distribution
  • Container stuffing & de-stuffing of FCL and LCL cargo
  • Value-added services: kitting, labelling and returns management

Cross-Border Capabilities

If your products move between Canada and the U.S., our GTA facilities offer customs-compliant warehousing and multi-carrier distribution. We coordinate duty-paid options, brokerage and final mile delivery so your customers receive shipments faster.
