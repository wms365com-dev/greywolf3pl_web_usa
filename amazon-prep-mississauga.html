Amazon FBA Prep in Mississauga

Our Mississauga facility prepares your products for FBA with inspection, labelling and bundling services that ensure compliance with Amazon's requirements.

Why Choose a Mississauga 3PL?

Amazon FBA sellers need a reliable partner to prepare, inspect and label inventory according to Amazon's strict guidelines. Grey Wolf's Mississauga facility specialises in FBA prep services, ensuring your products meet all requirements before reaching Amazon's fulfillment centres. We inspect goods upon arrival, apply FNSKU labels, bundle or multipack items as needed and ensure packaging meets Amazon's standards.

Our team handles storage, order tracking and shipment creation within Seller Central. We co-ordinate transportation to Amazon warehouses or directly to your customers, saving you time and reducing the risk of non-compliance. With our convenient location near Pearson Airport and major highways, we offer fast turnaround times for inbound shipments and Amazon removals.

Beyond FBA prep, we provide general eCommerce fulfillment, kitting, custom packaging and returns processing. We also support cross-border sellers by managing customs documentation and duty payments for shipments into Canada. Choose Grey Wolf in Mississauga to ensure your FBA inventory is prepped correctly and delivered on schedule.
