USA-to-Canada Warehousing & Fulfillment

Expand into Canada without the hassle. Our cross-border warehousing solutions handle customs, duty and distribution so your products reach Canadian customers quickly.

Why Choose a USA to Canada 3PL?

Entering the Canadian market from the U.S. requires more than just finding a warehouse. It demands an experienced partner who understands customs regulations, duty management and the nuances of cross-border logistics. Grey Wolf offers customs-bonded warehousing in Canada that allows you to store U.S. goods duty-deferred until orders are fulfilled. Our facilities are strategically located near major border crossings, ports and airports for efficient inbound and outbound moves.

We manage the entire cross-border process: receiving goods from the U.S., handling customs documentation and payment of applicable duties, and fulfilling orders to your Canadian customers. Our team ensures compliance with CBSA (Canada Border Services Agency) regulations and works closely with customs brokers to prevent delays. Real-time inventory visibility and order tracking keep you informed every step of the way.

In addition to storage and fulfillment, we offer value-added services such as FBA prep, kitting, relabelling and returns management. If you sell on multiple platforms, we integrate with your marketplaces and shopping carts to streamline order processing. By partnering with Grey Wolf for USA-to-Canada warehousing, you can focus on marketing and product development while we handle the complexities of cross-border distribution.

Our Services in USA to Canada

  • Customs-bonded warehousing and duty management
  • Cross-border fulfillment and final mile distribution in Canada
  • Customs documentation and brokerage co-ordination
  • FBA prep, kitting, relabelling and packaging
  • Returns processing and restocking for cross-border orders

Customs & Compliance

We stay up to date on Canadian import regulations and manage duty payments on your behalf, ensuring that your goods clear quickly and remain compliant.
