Shipping & Parcel Management

Our Shipping & Parcel Management service leverages an extensive network of national and regional carriers to optimize your deliveries. We compare rates in real time to select the most cost-effective shipping method for each package. Whether you need expedited, cost-effective or duty-paid options, we provide cross-border expertise to reach customers around the world while saving you money.

Key Features

  • Multi-carrier network with automated rate shopping.
  • Cross-border shipping solutions for Canada-U.S. shipments.
  • Expedited, cost-effective or duty-paid shipping options.
  • Parcel consolidation and tracking for efficiency.
  • Customizable shipping strategies tailored to your business.
