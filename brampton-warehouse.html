Warehousing in Brampton

Our Brampton facility delivers secure storage, order fulfillment and cross-docking, with easy access to the 401 and 410 highways.

Why Choose a Brampton 3PL?

Situated in the rapidly growing city of Brampton, our warehouse is ideal for businesses seeking cost-effective storage close to Toronto. With convenient access to Highways 401, 407 and 410, your goods move quickly through the GTA and to cross-border corridors. Our warehouse features secure racking and bulk storage options to accommodate a wide range of products and volumes.

Grey Wolf's Brampton team processes orders efficiently, leveraging advanced warehouse management software to track inventory in real time. Our pick and pack services ensure accurate order assembly, while our cross-docking reduces handling for shipments that require quick turnarounds. Whether you need seasonal overflow space or a dedicated fulfillment centre, we have the flexibility to scale with your business.

As part of our full 3PL offering, we handle container loading and unloading, value-added kitting, and returns processing. When shipping to the United States, we simplify customs clearance and coordinate deliveries through our network of carriers. Partner with us to streamline your supply chain in Brampton and beyond.

  • Pick, pack & ship for B2B and eCommerce orders
  • Cross-docking and transloading to reduce transit time
  • Container stuffing & de-stuffing services
