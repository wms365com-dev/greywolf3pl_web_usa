Warehousing in Vaughan

Our Vaughan facility delivers warehousing and fulfillment solutions near major highways, supporting businesses across York Region and beyond.

Why Choose a Vaughan 3PL?

Located at the intersection of Highway 400 and 407, Vaughan is a growing business hub with excellent transportation links. Grey Wolf's warehouse in Vaughan provides both rack and floor storage to accommodate diverse product types. Our advanced inventory systems ensure your stock is tracked accurately from receipt to dispatch.

We specialise in pick and pack services for eCommerce, retail and B2B orders. Our team optimises packing materials and carton sizes to reduce shipping costs and environmental impact. When speed is critical, our cross-docking operations move goods directly from incoming trucks to outgoing carriers, eliminating warehousing delays.

We also offer value-added services such as kitting, custom labelling and returns processing. If you're shipping across the border, our customs knowledge and broker network facilitate smooth exports. Choose Grey Wolf in Vaughan to improve your logistics efficiency and delight your customers.
