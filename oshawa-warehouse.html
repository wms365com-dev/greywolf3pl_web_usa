Warehousing in Oshawa

Our Oshawa facility provides scalable warehousing, order fulfillment and cross-docking for businesses across Durham Region.

Why Choose a Oshawa 3PL?

Oshawa is a growing manufacturing and logistics hub east of Toronto. Our warehouse supports businesses across Durham Region and Eastern Ontario with secure storage and efficient order fulfillment. Located near Highway 401, we enable quick access to the GTA and cross-border routes to the U.S.

Grey Wolf's Oshawa team handles receiving, storage, picking and shipping with precision and care. Our advanced inventory systems provide real-time tracking, while our pick and pack services ensure orders are processed accurately and shipped on time. Cross-docking and transloading services help expedite freight transfers, reducing dwell time and costs.

We also offer value-added services like kitting, labelling and returns management to enhance your supply chain. For cross-border shipments, we manage customs paperwork and carrier co-ordination. Partner with Grey Wolf in Oshawa to scale your logistics capabilities and serve your customers efficiently across Ontario and into the U.S.

Our Services in Oshawa

  • Secure storage with racking and bulk options
  • Pick, pack & ship for eCommerce and B2B orders
  • Cross-docking and transloading for faster distribution
  • Kitting, labelling and custom packaging
  • Returns management and refurbishment
