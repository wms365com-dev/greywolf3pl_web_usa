GTA Cross-Docking & Transloading

Reduce handling and transit times with our GTA cross-docking and transloading services, available across multiple facilities.

Why Choose a GTA 3PL?

Cross-docking streamlines your supply chain by transferring products directly from inbound to outbound carriers, eliminating the need for long-term storage. Grey Wolf operates cross-docking facilities across the GTA, enabling your goods to move quickly from ports, rail terminals and manufacturers to retailers, distributors and end customers.

Our team co-ordinates the arrival of inbound shipments, sorts and consolidates freight, and loads it onto outbound trucks efficiently. By minimising handling, cross-docking reduces the risk of damage and decreases storage costs. We also offer transloading services for switching freight between different modes of transportation (e.g., ocean container to truck or rail).

Whether you're shipping FCL, LCL or palletised freight, our cross-docking and transloading services help you meet tight delivery schedules. For cross-border moves, we manage customs clearance and duty payment so your shipments cross the border without delay.

Our Services in GTA

  • Transfer freight quickly between carriers and modes
  • Reduce handling, storage time and transportation costs
  • Consolidate and deconsolidate shipments in the GTA
  • Transloading between ocean, rail and truck
  • Customs clearance and brokerage for cross-border shipments

Cross-Border Expertise

Our cross-docking services integrate seamlessly with cross-border logistics. We handle documentation, duty payments and carrier co-ordination so your freight moves smoothly into and out of the U.S.
