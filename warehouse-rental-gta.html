Warehouse Rental Across the GTA

Need extra warehouse space in the GTA? Rent flexible storage with Grey Wolf and access our full suite of logistics services.

Why Choose a GTA 3PL?

Fluctuations in inventory levels, seasonal spikes and special projects often require additional warehouse space. Instead of committing to a long-term lease, Grey Wolf offers flexible warehouse rental options across the GTA. You can rent secure space in our modern facilities for as long as you need - weeks, months or longer - with the ability to scale up or down as your business demands.

Our rental customers gain access to more than just space. We provide racking, handling equipment and support from experienced warehouse staff. You can also leverage our pick and pack, cross-docking and transportation services to streamline your operations. Our facilities are located near major highways, giving you easy access to customers and carriers throughout the GTA.

For businesses shipping to the U.S., we integrate customs brokerage and duty payment services to simplify cross-border logistics. When you rent space with Grey Wolf, you gain a flexible logistics partner dedicated to meeting your needs without the overhead of managing your own facility.

  • Short-term and long-term warehouse rental options
  • Secure racking, equipment and handling support
  • Integration with pick & pack and cross-docking services
  • Locations across the GTA near major highways
  • Customs compliance and cross-border support
