FAQ | USA Vendors Entering Canada

Answers to the questions most vendors ask before shipping north.

Do I need a Canadian company to sell in Canada?

Not always. Many USA vendors start by importing inventory into Canada and fulfilling locally. We'll walk you through common setup options.

Who acts as the Importer of Record (IOR)?

It depends on your structure and broker setup. We guide the right setup so imports clear correctly and paperwork matches your channel.

What about duties, taxes, and GST/HST?

Product-based. Classification and value drive costs. We coordinate with brokers to reduce delays and surprise fees.

Can you handle returns within Canada?

Yes. We can receive returns, inspect, restock, and follow your SOP for disposition.

Do you support B2B wholesale shipments?

Yes - full case/pallet shipping, retailer prep, labeling, appointments, and LTL coordination.

Can you ship across all provinces?

Yes. Parcel and LTL options are available nationwide depending on size, speed, and budget.
