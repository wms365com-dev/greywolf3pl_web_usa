FAQ

Common questions about Canadian Tire support.

Can Grey Wolf support seasonal retail surges?

Yes. We can support ongoing retailer replenishment, seasonal spikes, and one-off projects that need staging, label control, and outbound prep.

Do you provide the EDI platform?

No. Grey Wolf supports the warehouse and freight-prep side of retailer compliance and coordinates with your EDI provider or internal team.

Can you correct label or pallet issues before the load moves?

Yes. We can help with relabeling, repalletizing, count checks, and freight cleanup before outbound delivery.
