FAQs

QuestionAnswer
What is a 3PL and how does it work?A 3PL (third-party logistics) provider manages warehousing, fulfillment and transportation on your behalf, leveraging their expertise and technology to streamline your supply chain.
How does using a 3PL save money?3PLs offer economies of scale and negotiate better shipping rates, helping you reduce operating costs and improve profit margins. You also avoid the capital expenses of building and maintaining your own facilities.
Do 3PLs provide real-time inventory visibility?Yes. Leading 3PLs use advanced warehouse management systems that provide live inventory and order status updates, ensuring transparency and enabling better planning.
Is a 3PL right for small businesses?Absolutely. Small and medium-sized businesses benefit from a 3PL's infrastructure and expertise without large upfront investments. Flexible pricing models let you scale services as needed.
What services should I look for in an Ontario 3PL?Key services include warehousing, eCommerce fulfillment, cross-docking, container stuffing/de-stuffing, co-packing, inventory management and returns processing. Choose a provider whose capabilities align with your products and channels.
How do 3PLs handle returns?Many 3PLs offer returns management, handling inspection, restocking, refunds and exchanges efficiently. This improves customer experience and reduces administrative overhead.
Can a 3PL support my cross-border shipping needs?Yes. A good 3PL has cross-border expertise and customs partnerships to move goods seamlessly between Canada and the U.S., offering options like duty-paid or expedited shipping.
What is cross-docking and why is it beneficial?Cross-docking transfers goods directly from incoming to outgoing transport with minimal storage. It speeds up distribution, reduces handling and lowers costs.
What is container stuffing/de-stuffing?Container stuffing involves loading goods efficiently into containers for transport. De-stuffing is unloading, tallying and organizing goods upon arrival. Both processes maximize space and reduce damage.
How do I get started with a 3PL in Ontario?Contact prospective providers to discuss your needs, request quotes and inquire about technology, service offerings, locations and references. Visit facilities if possible and compare proposals to find the best fit.
