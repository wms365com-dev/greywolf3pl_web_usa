Frequently asked questions

What is a 3PL and how does it work?

A 3PL partner manages warehousing, fulfillment and transportation support on your behalf. Instead of building everything internally, you use the provider's space, labor, systems and shipping network to keep inventory moving.

How does using a 3PL save money?

You avoid the cost of building your own operation, gain access to shared infrastructure and can often improve shipping efficiency through better carrier options and more flexible handling.

Do you provide inventory visibility?

Yes. Grey Wolf focuses on practical communication and operational visibility so clients understand what is in storage, what is moving and where exceptions need attention.

Is a 3PL only for large businesses?

No. Smaller and mid-sized businesses often benefit the most because they get access to experienced logistics support without the upfront cost of standing up a full internal team and warehouse footprint.

What services should I look for in an Ontario 3PL?

Start with warehousing, order fulfillment, cross-docking, container handling, shipping support, retail prep, inventory visibility and returns handling. Then make sure the provider can actually support your real operating flow.

Can Grey Wolf support cross-border shipping?

Yes. Grey Wolf supports cross-border logistics workflows and shipping coordination for businesses moving product between Canada and the U.S.

What is cross-docking and why is it useful?

Cross-docking shifts freight from inbound to outbound flow with minimal storage time. It helps reduce handling, improve speed and keep product moving when time matters.

Do you handle co-packing or retail prep projects?

Yes. Grey Wolf can support labeling, assembly, repacking and other value-added handling for projects that need more than storage alone.

How do I get started?

The best first step is to share your product type, approximate volume, service needs and timing. From there, Grey Wolf can scope the workflow and recommend the right setup.
