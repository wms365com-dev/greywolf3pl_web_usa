Warehousing in Oakville

Our Oakville facility serves clients across Halton Region with efficient warehousing and fulfillment close to major highways and ports.

Why Choose a Oakville 3PL?

Oakville's strategic position between Toronto and Hamilton makes it an ideal hub for businesses looking to serve the Greater Toronto and Hamilton Area (GTHA). Our warehouse, located just off the QEW and Highway 403, offers easy access to ports, airports and border crossings. We provide flexible storage solutions, from palletized racking to floor space, to accommodate your inventory needs.

Partnering with Grey Wolf means benefiting from efficient pick and pack operations, real-time inventory visibility and customised packaging options. Our team integrates seamlessly with your order management systems, ensuring that orders are processed accurately and shipped quickly. We also offer cross-docking to minimize storage time and accelerate deliveries.

In addition to core warehousing services, we provide kitting, labelling and returns management. For clients shipping to the U.S., our expertise in customs compliance simplifies the export process. Whether you're an eCommerce brand or a growing manufacturer, our Oakville facility supports your logistics requirements with reliability and care.

Our Services in Oakville

  • Pallet and bulk storage with flexible term options
  • Pick, pack & ship services across Halton and the GTHA
  • Cross-docking and transloading for time-sensitive freight
  • Kitting, labelling and packaging customisation
  • Returns processing and refurbishment services

Border & Port Access

Our proximity to the Port of Hamilton and major border crossings means we can efficiently handle imports, exports and customs clearance for clients shipping internationally.
