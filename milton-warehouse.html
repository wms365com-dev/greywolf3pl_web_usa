Warehousing in Milton

Our Milton facility serves businesses across Halton and Southwestern Ontario with secure storage, efficient order fulfillment and cross-docking services.

Why Choose a Milton 3PL?

Milton's location along the 401 corridor provides quick access to the GTA, Hamilton and Southwestern Ontario. Our warehouse offers flexible racking and bulk storage to accommodate products ranging from consumer goods to industrial materials. With 24/7 security and modern inventory systems, we maintain the integrity of your goods while enabling real-time visibility.

We provide pick and pack services for eCommerce and retail clients, optimising packaging and shipping methods to reduce costs and transit times. Our cross-docking and transloading operations keep freight moving, whether your shipments are headed to other Canadian provinces or the U.S.

In addition, we offer kitting, labelling and returns management to support your full supply chain. Our Milton team handles customs documentation and co-ordinates with carriers for cross-border deliveries. With Grey Wolf as your Milton logistics partner, you can expand your reach across Ontario and beyond.

Our Services in Milton

  • Flexible racking and bulk storage solutions
  • Pick, pack & ship with optimized packaging
  • Cross-docking and transloading to minimise handling
  • Kitting, labelling and custom packaging
  • Returns processing and refurbishment

Cross-Border Expertise

Our Milton facility is strategically located for exports to the U.S. We provide brokerage coordination, duty payments and final mile delivery through trusted carriers.
