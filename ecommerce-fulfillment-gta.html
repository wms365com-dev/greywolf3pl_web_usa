eCommerce Fulfillment Across the GTA

Delight your customers with fast, accurate eCommerce fulfillment across the GTA. We handle pick, pack and ship so you can focus on growing your brand.

Why Choose a GTA 3PL?

Fast delivery and accurate order fulfillment are essential to eCommerce success. Grey Wolf's network of GTA warehouses is purpose-built for online merchants, providing real-time inventory visibility, efficient pick and pack operations and seamless integration with your shopping cart and marketplace platforms.

Our fulfillment team receives your goods, stores them in secure racking and processes orders as soon as they come in. We use optimised packing materials to reduce dimensional weight charges and protect your products in transit. Same-day and next-day shipping options within the GTA ensure your customers receive orders quickly, while our cross-docking services keep freight moving for larger shipments.

In addition to fulfillment, we handle kitting, custom packaging, labeling and returns management. Our technology allows you to monitor orders, track inventory and generate reports in real time. For cross-border orders to the U.S., we handle customs paperwork and co-ordinate carriers so your customers receive orders without surprises.

We support eCommerce brands shipping to the U.S. with customs documentation, duty payments and carrier co-ordination. Your international customers enjoy a smooth delivery experience.
